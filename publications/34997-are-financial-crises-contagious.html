Are Financial Crises Contagious?

Author

Elise S. Brezis

Publication Details

Department of Economics, Bar-Ilan University, 2024 Jul, p. 22

Abstract

This working paper investigates the mechanisms through which financial crises spread across countries and regions. The study examines contagion effects in financial markets and analyzes the factors that determine the transmission of financial instability.

Keywords

Financial Crises, Contagion, Financial Markets, International Finance, Crisis Transmission
